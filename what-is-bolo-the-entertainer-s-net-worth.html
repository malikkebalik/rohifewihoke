The Real Housewives of Atlanta recently aired its most talked-about episode of the Bravo series. During a COVID-19 friendly bachelorette party, viewers watched as the cast enjoyed Bolo the Entertainer’s performance. The exotic dancer danced Cynthia Bailey’s bachelorette party in summer 2020. 

Since Bolo’s debut, rumors have swirled about what went down at the bachelorette party. Although the publicity put him in the spotlight, the dancer has worked in the entertainment industry for several years. RHOA fans might not know what is Bolo the Entertainer’s net worth, and how does he feel about his recent publicity?

Bolo the Entertainer worked as an actor before his ‘The Real Housewives of Atlanta’ appearance 

Bolo the Entertainer’s real name is Michael Bolwaire. According to Primal Information, the dancer is from Cleveland, Ohio, but moved to Atlanta for his career. In addition to dancing, Bolo works as an actor and model. Before his debut on RHOA, Bolo appeared in Chocolate City. The male stripper drama also starred Tyson Beckford and DeRay Davis, per IMDb. He was also in Vivica A. Fox’s reality show, Vivica’s Black Magic, in 2017. 

Bolo the Entertainer’s Instagram account is also quite impressive. While he’s not verified on the social media app, he currently has 247,000 Instagram followers. Additionally, the dancer told Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen in March 2021 that he gained notoriety before his RHOA appearance. Bolo also said he received a plethora of direct messages from fans of the show shortly after his scenes aired. However, the attention is something he got after other projects.

“I’ve done a couple of different projects, so I’m kind of accustomed to, you know, a lot of people at a time. I did a couple of other movies in the past, and just a couple things that I’ve been doing that it was just like, hey, sh*t, I’m used to that attention.” 

The net worth of Bolo the Entertainer since ‘RHOA’

Bolo the Entertainer’s net worth is reported at $1 million. Although he’s only done a few television roles, the 31-year-old exotic dancer reportedly earns money from his YouTube page, which he launched in 2008. 

Additionally, he told Cohen in March 2021 that his OnlyFans page has profited from the recent rumors from Bailey’s bachelorette party. The subscription-based website allows people to pay a monthly fee to receive adult content directly from their favorite content producers, ranging from photos to videos to online chats.

“It’s doing pretty good. It’s doing a lot better than it was about two to three weeks ago,” he said of his OnlyFans page. 

What ‘RHOA’ stars Porsha Williams and Tanya Sam have said about the Bolo the Entertainer rumors

In Oct. 2020, Page Six reported that two members of the RHOA cast allegedly slept with Bolo the Entertainer. The report named reality stars Porsha Williams and Tanya Sam as the culprits when the activities occurred. However, Sam, who is engaged to Paul Judge, vehemently denied the allegations. 

“I will address this topic now and will not entertain this absurdity further,” she shared on Instagram. “The rumor circulating about me is NOT TRUE.”

As far as Williams, the mother of one decided to laugh off the rumors. While she didn’t address Bolo or the alleged threesome directly, she recalled having a “good time” at the bachelorette party, per Entertainment Tonight.
