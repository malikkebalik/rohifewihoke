What is Liannet Borrego's Net Worth?

Liannet Borrego is a Cuban actress, model and dancer who has a net worth of $2 million. Liannet Borrego was born January 28, 1988 in La Habana, Cuba. As a young girl, Liannet Borrego studied dance and at age five was a ballet and flamenco dancer. She studied both dancing and acting until she moved to the United States and turned her focus on acting. Her awards include Miss Cuban American Teen (2003), Miss Teen Face Best Cuban American (2003), Miss Hispanidad (Princess, 2005), Beauty Contest Sabado Gigante (2003), Dancing Contest Sabado Gigante (2002) and The Competition, Most Beautiful Daughter (2002).
