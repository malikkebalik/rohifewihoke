Did He Marry Neslie Ricasa?

Does Joey Chestnut have kids? No, he does not have any kids. He proposed to Neslie Ricasa in 2014, but they never married.

Joey Chestnut is an American athlete and competitive eater.

He holds the top ranking in the world according to Major League Eating as of 2022. In preparation for competitions, he follows a training regimen involving consuming substantial food regularly.

He maximizes his food intake within shorter time frames by employing strategies like rapid chewing and swallowing without thoroughly chewing.

Similarly, Chestnut enjoys significant prominence in competitive eating and frequently appears in the media. His appearances include notable shows like The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon and The Today Show.

Moreover, he serves as a spokesperson for various food brands. Notably, on July 4, 2007, Chestnut emerged victorious in the 92nd Annual Nathan’s Hot Dog Eating Contest in New York City.

Joey Chestnut Kids And Wife: Did He Marry Neslie Ricasa?

Joey Chestnut does not have any kids. But Chestnut loves babies. At the end of 2022, he shared a photo on Instagram where he was holding a baby.

In the caption, Chestnut wrote, “Not my baby. Holding a beautiful baby makes you see things differently. Parents are like heroes.”

In the post’s comment section, he commented, “Raising the next generation should be seen as something to strive for and feel honored about, never as a burden.”

In 2014, he proposed to Neslie Ricasa, but they never married. They ended their relationship in 2015 and have not been romantically linked to anyone since then.

Chestnut and Ricasa met 2011 at a competitive eating event and started dating soon after. However, they announced their breakup in 2015, and the reasons for their split remain unknown.

The New York Post reported in July 2015 that Joey and Neslie had ended their relationship just weeks before their wedding. According to Joey, the demands of his competition schedule created difficulties in their relationship.

He mentioned that the frequent traveling caused strain, saying, “I definitely would say it was traveling. I was gone quite a bit.” Also, he described the decision to part ways as mutual, stating, “It was mutual. It just didn’t work.”

However, being single gave Joey a greater incentive to focus on his upcoming competition.

He admitted that returning to the place where he proposed for that year’s Nathan’s Famous Hot Dog-Eating Contest might be challenging, but he saw it as an opportunity to concentrate on his performance.

Since parting ways with Ricasa, Joey Chestnut has not been involved in romantic relationships. Currently, he is focusing on his career in competitive eating and his business ventures. Additionally, he serves as a spokesperson for several food brands.

Who Is Joey Chestnut Ex-Girlfriend Neslie Ricasa?

Currently, Neslie Ricasa works as a financial analyst at Thomas Foods International.

After completing her studies, Ricasa obtained a bachelor’s degree from “San Jose State University.” Before graduating, she worked as an accounting assistant at EK Health Services in August 2014.

Chestnut and Neslie started their relationship in 2011. After dating for three years, Chestnut proposed to Neslie before the 2014 Nathan’s Hot Dog Eating Contest.

They remained engaged and planned to get married the following year, but unfortunately, they broke up before their wedding.

Currently, Neslie is married to her husband, Drew Fly. They have two children and live in their home in San Jose.
