Tom Sandoval Allegedly Told Fans Not To Vote For Ariana Madix On 'Dancing With The Stars'

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Credit: Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for MTV

It seems like Tom Sandoval is still hung up on Ariana Madix. An anonymous source spilled all of the tea about their visit to TomTom in Los Angeles, and it turns out Sandoval was telling fans to vote against Ariana on Dancing with the Stars. 

They revealed some more details about their night out with the Vanderpump Rules star.

Tom Sandoval allegedly didn’t want to see his ex-girlfriend make it to the end on her Dancing with the Stars run.

According to a Reddit user, Tom was telling girls at his restaurant to not vote for Ariana Madix.

“Sandoval hitting on girls then asking them to not vote for Ariana on Dancing with the Stars..?!” the user questioned.

Someone in a DWTS Reddit thread spilled all of the details about their night with Tom.

“So I had recently gone to Tom Tom’s with some friends and Tom was there,” the anonymous user started. “I am not proud of it because I am an Ariana fan but since he was hitting on us and giving free drinks we acted like we were team Tom Sandoval.”

The anonymous user alleged that Tom told them to vote for anyone other than Ariana.

“The thing is he eventually mentioned that if we were to vote for Harry [Jowsey] at the time or Alyson [Hannigan] but not Ariana and show him he would hit us up,” they continued.

The user noted they “thought he was joking because he was drunk,” but they ultimately decided to play along.

“Now a friend of ours was telling us that he did the same thing when she was there with other people,” they said. “I am wondering if this happened to other women and men too? I don’t understand why he hates her since she didn’t do anything it was all him.”
