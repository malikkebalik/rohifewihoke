“Turn of the Tide” Actor José Condessa Wiki | Bio

José Condessa (age: 26 years; born on June 6, 1997) is a Portuguese actor known for his impressive TV career. He gained prominence from his performances in shows like “Pôr do Sol,” “Bem Me Quer,” “Until Life Do Us Part” and “Run for Your Lives” although many people recognize him as Rafa from the 2015 production “Jardins Proibidos.”

Condessa has also worked on many movies, including some very popular productions like “O Som Que Desce na Terra,” “Salgueiro Maia – The Implicated,” and “Gabriel.” These days this handsome Portuguese star is hovering in entertainment world news because of his recent work “Turn of the Tide,” which came on Netflix on May 26, 2023.

Augusto Fraga’s “Turn of the Tide” is an action thriller that revolves around 5 friends and a sunken boat filled with cocaine. If you want to watch a show with a completely out-of-the-box storyline, you should definitely watch “Turn of the Tide.”

How Old is José Condessa?

On June 6, 1997, José Condessa (age 26) was born to his father, Jorge Condessa, and mother, Delfina Esteves, in Lisbon, Portugal. According to sources, Condessa was only four years old when he stepped onto the stage.

At that time, he accompanied his father, who is also an actor; upon asking, Jose confessed that his dad played an important role in carving his acting career. Certain that he wanted to be an actor, Condessa entered the Cascais Professional Theater School in 2012.

His professional theatre debut came a year later at Teatro Experimental de Cascais, which led to his selection for the role of Rafael in “Jardins Proibidos.”

José Condessa’s Acting Career

Condessa’s performance as Rafa “Jardins Proibidos” helped him leave a significant mark on the Portuguese audience, which helped him earn a role in his second TV show, “Santa Bárbara,” which came out in 2016.

The same year he starred in the Nuno Rocha-directed movie titled “A Mãe é que Sabe,” where he got to share the screen with Filipa Areosa, Maria João Abreu, Catarina Rebelo, and Adriano Carvalho.

In the next few years, Jose’s career progressed immensely as he got to be in productions like “Filha da Lei,” “Rainha das Flores,” “Ministério do Tempo,” “Espelho d’Água,” “Gabriel,” “Cenas de Família” and “Valor da Vida.”

In 2020, José appeared in a comedy-drama, “Run for Your Lives.” It was a Daniel Ortiz-directed show featuring talented stars like Deborah Secco, Gabriela Moreyra, Betty Faria, and Flávia Alessandra.

Who is José Condessa’s Girlfriend?

According to sources, Condessa has been dating Bárbara Branco for many. Their love story commenced in 2014 when fate brought them together at the renowned Cascais Professional Theater School.

As actors bound by their shared love for the dramatic arts, Condessa and Bárbara discovered a profound connection and began dating. Both actors are very open about their relationship and frequently share their photos on Instagram.

Recently José and Branco worked together on a show, “O Crime do Padre Amaro”; Condessa played Padre Amaro, whereas Bárbara appeared as Amélia.
