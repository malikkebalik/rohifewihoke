Who's the Richest Backstreet Boy?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

The Backstreet Boys are back again. Here’s the group’s net worth and which Backstreet Boy is the richest.

How they make their money

The Backstreet Boys earn money through several income streams. Among their income sources is touring and album sales. On March 1, 2017, the group began a Las Vegas residency at Zappos Theater (formerly The AXIS Theater) in the Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino. According to FOX5 Vegas – KVV, Backstreet Boys: Larger Than Life was the fastest-selling residency in Las Vegas in history at the time.

Another source of income is product endorsements. In February 2019, the group appeared in a Superbowl ad for Doritos’ new Flamin’ Hot Nacho, alongside artist Chance the Rapper.

Nick Carter’s net worth

Nick Carter started his entertainment career as an actor. He made his acting debut in the 1990 TV movie Judgment. After that, he had a small part in the 1990 film Edward Scissorhands. Other acting credits include American Dreams, 8 Simple Rules, The Hollow, and 90210. Nick Carter has a net worth of $35 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Kevin Richardson’s net worth

Kevin Richardson is the cousin of fellow Backstreet Boy Brian Littrell. He started out as an actor, making his acting debut in the 1991 film My Girl. In 2009, he appeared in the film Love Takes Wing. Other acting credits include The Casserole Club, Unwound, and The Bloody Indulgent. Furthermore, Richardson has modeled for Versace and Vogue. Kevin Richardson has a net worth of $40 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Howie Dorough’s net worth

Howie Dorough made his acting debut in 1989 in the film Parenthood. He has also appeared in The Mummy; Roswell; Sabrina, The Teenage Witch; Constellation; and This is the End. Howie Dorough has a net worth of $45 million.

AJ McLean’s net worth

AJ McLean began acting at the age of 8. He made his acting debut in the 1986 movie Truth or Dare: A Critical Madness. In 1991, he had a part in the pilot of the TV series Hi Honey, I’m Home. Additional acting credits include Olive Juice, Static Shock, and Dead 7. AJ McLean has a net worth of $45 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Brian Littrell’s net worth

Brian Littrell is also a Christian recording artist. He released his debut solo album Welcome Home in May 2006 through the Sony BMG and Christian music label Reunion Records. The album reached the No. 74 spot on the Billboard 200 and the No. 3 spot on the Christian Albums chart. The single “Welcome Home (You),” reached the No. 1 spot on the Christian Songs chart and resulted in a Dove Award for best video of the year. Brian Littrell has a net worth of $45 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.
