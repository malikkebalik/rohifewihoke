Centersquare Investment Management Holdings, Inc. (Plymouth Meeting)

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Centersquare Investment Management Holdings, Inc. is a large advisory firm based in Plymouth Meeting. It manages $577.44 million of regulatory assets for 7 client accounts. It has been registered with the SEC as an adviser since 2014 and has operated in the jurisdictions of California, Pennsylvania, Texas, and Washington.

Centersquare Investment Management Holdings provides portfolio management for pooled investment vehicles and portfolio management for businesses and institutional clients. It doesn't provide financial planning services to its clients. On top of advisory services, the firm doesn't engage in other business activities.

Centersquare Investment Management Holdings: assets under management

The assets under management (AUM) of Centersquare Investment Management Holdings, Inc. is $577.44 million. The firm's AUM has gone down by -30% since the beginning of its operation, and the company doesn't manage any assets belonging to foreign clients.


The firm manages $187.26 million in discretionary accounts and $390.19 million in non-discretionary accounts. On average, the discretionary accounts where all decisions are made on behalf of the client hold $37.45 million each. Non-discretionary accounts, where clients actively manage their portfolios, generally hold around $195.09 million.

Centersquare Investment Management Holdings has custody of $561.48 million in cash and securities for 6 clients in total but doesn’t act as a qualified custodian.

Fees & Commissions

The advisory firm has several streams of revenue. Clients pay Centersquare Investment Management Holdings, Inc. for their services and products based on a percentage of assets under management, fixed fees, and performance-based fees. Compensation based on assets under management promotes a long-term relationship between a client and the adviser and helps align mutual interests. These fees don’t include brokerage commissions, interest, taxes, and other account expenses.
