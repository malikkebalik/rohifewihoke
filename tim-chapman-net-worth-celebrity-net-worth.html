What is Tim Chapman's Net Worth?

Tim Chapman is an American retired bounty hunter and reality television personality who has a net worth of $3 million. Tim Chapman was born in Ventura, California in May 1965. Chapman is best known for his role on the A&E reality television series Dog the Bounty Hunter. Chapman is a third generation bondsman and made his first civilian arrest at 14 years old. While working at his mother's bail bond business Tim met Duane "Dog" Chapman although there is no relation. Chapman joined Dog, Dog's girlfriend Beth Smith, and Dog's son Leland to start Da Kine Bail Bonds in Hawaii.

Chapman appeared on the first four seasons of Dog the Bounty Hunter and was on the show from 2003 to 2012. Chapman, Dog, and Leland were arrested by U.S. Marshalls in 2006 facing charges of deprivation of liberty from charges regarding serial rapist Andrew Luster in 2003. Tim was released on bail of $10,000 and the charges were later dropped. Chapman was charged with terroristic threatening in 2008 as well as indecent exposure. He was acquitted of indecent exposure and the threatening charges were dropped. Chapman also acted in the movie Empire of Ash III in 1989.
