The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers for Wednesday, May 10, tease that Victoria (Amelia Heinle) will try to butter Billy (Jason Thompson) up. When Billy drops by Victoria’s place, he’ll want to set up a visitation schedule for the kids. Victoria will insist that’s not necessary. She’ll note that Billy is welcome to come over whenever he wants.

Later, Victoria will praise Billy’s idea for a new ad campaign. Cane (Daniel Goddard) will be irked when he realizes Victoria is still playing favorites. Lily (Christel Khalil) won’t approve of Cane’s constant negativity. Since she’s getting a shot at a national commercial, she’ll feel like Cane should just be happy for her.

Y&R fans can expect another shouting match for Cane and Billy. The work environment is quite hostile for these two. Meanwhile, The Young and the Restless spoilers say Phyllis (Gina Tognoni) will bump into Victoria. She’ll ask for a moment of her time to work through their recent conflict.

Victoria will get snappy as she brags about Billy’s plan to stop by her house on a nightly basis. He’ll be tucking in their children and strengthening their bond. Victoria will point out that she’s also headed to Los Angeles with Billy for a business trip. She’ll get on the elevator and leave Phyllis to mull over all this information.

At Newman Enterprises, Victor (Eric Braeden) will give Scott (Daniel Hall) and Abby (Melissa Ordway) some instructions. Once Abby exits, Scott will ask Victor to explain the situation. He won’t understand why Victor has doubts about Abby’s competence. Victor will simply suggest that ambition can lead to some strange behavior.

At Jabot, Abby will blow off some steam in Jack’s office. She’ll give him the news that Victor is hiring Scott. Jack will urge Abby to play this carefully. After Ashley (Eileen Davidson) gets updates, she’ll head over to give Victor a piece of her mind. Scott and Vic will do their best to provide a convincing argument. Ashley will relent, but she’ll contend that Scott had better not make Abby seem inept.

Across town, Jordan (Darnell Kirkwood) will speak with Chelsea (Melissa Claire Egan) at her penthouse. He’ll admit that he couldn’t find out where Chloe’s (Elizabeth Hendrickson) fake passport came from. Later, Nick (Joshua Morrow) will realize that Chelsea still isn’t letting of her Chloe search. She’ll ask for Nick’s help, but he’ll insist that they need to back off.

Chelsea won’t appreciate Nick’s lack of support. She’ll throw him out as she prepares to do this on her own. It’s clear that Chelsea isn’t giving up on justice. Will she find the answers she’s seeking? We’ll keep you posted as other Young and the Restless details emerge.
