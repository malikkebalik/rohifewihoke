Briana DeJesus's Sister Brittany Thinks She Could Marry Devoin Austin

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Credit: MTV

Are there wedding bells in the future for Briana DeJesus and Devoin Austin? Her sister Brittany DeJesus seems to think it’s in the cards one day. Given their history and their recent flirtations, there’s a good reason that they’re talking about it!

Going to the chapel?

Brittany DeJesus sounds pretty ready for her sister Briana DeJesus to marry Devoin Austin, her first baby daddy.

On the recent Teen Mom: The Next Chapter reunion, they discussed the former couple reuniting and taking the next step.

Devoin has been praised by fans in the past for the way he includes both of Briana’s daughters despite only being the father to her eldest Nova.

The topic of marriage arose on the reunion when Dr. Drew Pinsky asked Briana how her plan to have her sister adopt her youngest child Stella.

The former Teen Mom 3 star has struggled with her coparenting relationship with Luis Hernandez since she gave birth to their daughter together in 2017.

Earlier this season, the 29-year-old sat down with Brittany and their mom Roxanne to discuss her issues with Stella’s dad.

“So as you guys know, Luis hasn’t really been, like, around,” she begins in the clip. “He’s been really inconsistent. I think Stella deserves more.”

That’s when Briana raised her unconventional proposal to the rest of the family.

“There’s a thing where, like, a close relative can adopt Stella,” Briana explained. The Teen Mom 3 alum then asked, “So I was wondering if Brittany would be able to do that for me.”

“Yeah I will,” her sister responded without hesitation, jokingly adding, “I’m a mother. No drama.”

Roxanne, however, had some concerns of her own, stating, “I think that you should not cross Luis out so fast.”
